Elon Musk

Biography

Elon Musk is an entrepreneur and business magnate. He is the founder, CEO, and lead designer of SpaceX; co-founder, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; and co-founder and CEO of Neuralink.

Achievements

  • Co-founded PayPal and sold it to eBay for $1.5 billion
  • Founded SpaceX with the goal of colonizing Mars
  • Launched the Falcon Heavy, the world's most powerful rocket
  • Created the Tesla Model S, the first electric car to win Motor Trend's Car of the Year award
  • Co-founded OpenAI to promote artificial intelligence research

Quotes

"I think it's possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary."
"If something is important enough, even if the odds are against you, you should still do it."
